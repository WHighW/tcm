PS4, PS5: December Free Games Confirmed

Godfall will be available to users of both consoles

PlayStation announced this Wednesday (1) which are the December free games for PS Plus subscribers. Godfall: Challenger Edition tops the list, joined by Mortal Shell and LEGO DC Super Villains. All titles will be available from December 7th.

Check out the descriptions shared by PlayStation:

Godfall: Challenger Edition (PS5 and PS4): "Godfall is a loot and fighting game set in a brilliant fantasy universe filled with heroic knights and arcane magic. Embark on the adventure of this fantasy action RPG that uses melee combat High-impact third-person games to engage players as they search for loot, don legendary armor sets, and defeat wicked enemies. The Challenger Edition focuses on three modes: Lightbringer, Dreamstones and Ascended Tower of Trials. All are endgame-level content, but you'll immediately be equipped with a variety of lethal weapons and skill points, which allow you to team up with up to two other players in co-op."

LEGO DC Super-Villains (PS4): "It's good to be bad… Embark on an all-new DC Lego adventure to become the greatest villain that ever lived in the universe. Players will create an all-new supervillain and play like him throughout the game, unleashing evil and wreaking havoc in an action-packed story. Set in an open-world experience in the DC universe, the Justice League has disappeared, leaving Earth's protection to its peers, who have proclaimed themselves the 'Union of Justice'".

Mortal Shell (PS4): "Mortal Shell is a relentless, deep action RPG that tests your sanity and resilience in a shattered world. As the rest of humanity withers and rots, fiery enemies swarm through the ruins. With survival demanding conscience , precision and superior instincts, they spare no one. Find hidden shrines of devoted followers and discover their true purpose."

Three PS VR bonus games are also confirmed: The Persistence, The Walking Dead: Saints & Sinners and Until You Fall. If you haven't redeemed the PS Plus games for November, please be aware that they will only remain available until December 6th.
