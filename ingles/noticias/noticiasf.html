TGA: It Takes Two named Game of the Year at The Game Awards

Hazelight Game also won Best Multiplayer and Best Family Game

The moment you’ve all been waiting for, Game of The Year! A massive congratulations to It Takes Two @hazelightgames + @ea + ​​@josef_fares for your big win! #TheGameAwards pic.twitter.com/3o2kGm8qHm

— The Game Awards (@thegameawards) 10 de dezembro de 2021

It Takes Two was named Game of the Year by The Game Awards 2021.

The game from the Hazelight studio won three awards in total, having also won the Best Multiplayer and Best Family Game categories.

Another game that won three statuettes during the event was Forza Horizon 5, winner of Best Sport/Racing Game, Best Audio Design and Accessibility Innovation.

Deathloop, in turn, won in the categories of Best Direction and Best Art Direction. Kena: Bridge of Spirits and Final Fantasy XIV, meanwhile, also took two awards each.

Among the others indicateds to the Game of the Year, Resident Evil Village and Metroid Dread each won - Best Performance and Best Action Adventure Game, respectively - while Ratchet & Clank: In Another Dimension and Psychonauts 2 took none category.
