PS4, PS5: Free PS Plus games from December may have been leaked

The list includes Morta Shell and LEGO DC Super-Villains

Player theory was based on the criterion that the 7th of September falls on the first Tuesday of December, which is when new games are usually made available. But apparently this was a flawed theory and the aforementioned game will not be among those available.

A Dealabs user named Billbill-Kun — who even leaked the September, October, and November games and games distributed to Xbox Live Gold subscribers — told the internet that December's PS Plus games will be:

  • Godfall Challenger Edition (PS4/PS5)
  • Dead Shell (PS4)
  • LEGO DC Super-Villains (PS4)


In the case of Godfall Challenger Edition, it appears to be a version of the game that has not yet been officially announced by Sony.

It is worth remembering that this list is about rumors and it is necessary to wait for the official confirmation of Sony regarding the games of December of its subscription service.
