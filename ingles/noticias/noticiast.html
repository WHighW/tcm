The Game Awards 2021: See the full list of winners

Awards take place on December 9th and celebrate the biggest games of the year

One of the most prestigious awards in the entire gaming industry, The Game Awards 2021 takes place this Thursday (9) and will award the biggest and most acclaimed games of the last year.

Check out the indicated and (future) winners of the ceremony below, which features titles such as Deathloop, It Takes Two, Resident Evil Village and more in the running for GOTY.

Game of the year

  • Deathloop (Arkane Studios/Bethesda)
  • It Takes Two (Hazelight Studios/EA)
  • Metroid Dread (Mercury Steam/Nintendo)
  • Psychonauts 2 (Double Fine/Xbox Game Studios)
  • Ratchet & Clank: Rift Apart (Insomniac Games/SIE
  • Resident Evil Village (Capcom)
