Google Play Releases List of Best 2021 Games

Titles like League of Legends: Wild Rift and MARVEL Future Revolution make part of the list...

Google announced this Tuesday (Nov 30) the best apps and games from the Brazilian store in 2021. They were selected by a Google editorial team, which takes into account criteria such as quality and ratings from the public.

In games, the Play Store has seen a wave of independent developers creating imaginative and personal gaming experiences.

With a wave of gaming innovation, the big highlights of competitive gaming were Rogue Land, MARVEL Future Revolution, Northgard, League of Legends: Wild Rift, Suspects: Mystery Mansion.

In the indie category, developer George Batchelor brought out Bird Alone, which challenges the player to become friends with the loneliest bird in the world.

In addition, Donut County also entered the category and is physics-based puzzle game developed by Annapurna Interactive and you play as a hole in the ground that is always growing.

Check out the list of winners below:

The most competitive

Rogue Land: Dynamic 3D game in a world of fantasy and monsters;

MARVEL Future Revolution: first mobile RPG based on the Marvel universe;

Northgard: strategy game based on the famous Norse Mythology;

League of Legends: Wild Rift: mobile version one of the most famous games in the world;

Suspects: Mystery Mansion: Up to nine player online mystery solving game.

The most innovative

JanKenUP!: the famous online rock, paper and scissors game;

Inked: Solve puzzles with the identity of a Samurai;

Sixit: game with only six actions per turn that takes players to explore a forest and save their friends from the Great Storm;

Unmaze - an interactive fable of light and shadow: a visual novel inspired by Greek mythology where you play as Ariadne and need to guide Asterion and Theseus out of the maze.

The best indies

Donut County: Story-based physics puzzle game in which you play as an ever-growing hole in the ground;

Puzzling Peaks EXE: 2D platform strategy game full of puzzles;

My Friend Pedro: Ripe for Revenge: based on the computer game by Devolver Digital;

Bird Alone: ​​game of overcoming, adventure, music and life;

7 Billion Humans: game that allows the player to manage a world with 7 billion people.

The best casuals

Project Makeover: game that redesigns the characters by choosing clothes, hairstyles, makeup and even trendy furniture;

Disney POP TOWN: game of levels that, by solving challenging puzzles, you can collect costumes and build cities with Disney themes;

Crash Bandicoot: On the Run!: Along with Crash Bandicoot and his sister Coco, the player has a mission to save the multiverse!;

Cats in Time: the player must solve puzzles to save the kittens who traveled through a time machine and got stuck in the Middle Ages;

Switchcraft: Match 3 Magicians : Through the puzzle solving of hundreds of stages, the player walks to solve Lydia's disappearance in a plot that unfolds in a beautiful comic book.

Best for tablets

My Friend Pedro: Ripe for Revenge: Based on the computer game of the same name, this new game brings everything from its predecessor to mobile phones;

Chicken Police: Paint it Red!: an investigation game where the player takes on the role of a Chicken investigating crimes;

League of Legends: Wild Rift: the mobile version one of the most famous games in the world.
